# Feature: Content Coverage Indicator

**Date:** 2026-04-02
**Priority:** Quick win — can ship alongside current text pipeline

## Business Outcome
After AI structures a document, show the user what was captured vs. what was missed. Turns content loss from a silent failure into a transparent, recoverable interaction.

## What It Looks Like
On the preview step (Step 3), show:
- "We detected 12 topics in your document. 8 were included in your artifact."
- Checklist of detected topics with checkmarks (included) and gaps (not included)
- "Add missing sections" button that re-runs AI on just the missed content

## Why It Matters
Committee review scored content loss as the #1 product problem (5.5/10). Users uploading a strategy deck expect comprehensive coverage. This feature doesn't fix the loss — it makes it visible and actionable.
